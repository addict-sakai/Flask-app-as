Cases Where Insurance Benefits Will Be Paid

Insurance can be claimed only when accidents took place In Mt.FUJIPARAGLIDING.

※Insurance benefits will be payable only if the injured person visits a hospital on the day of the injury or the following day and obtains a medical certificate.

Cases Where Insurance Benefits Will Not Be Paid

Insurance will not be claimed if proper registration is not done at the reception counter.

※Regardless of the cause, insurance benefits will not be paid for cervical syndrome (so-called whiplash injury) or lower back pain without objective medical findings.

※insurance will not be paid also to the injury caused by the reasons listed in the following Items.

  • Ａ）If the intention was proved to be premeditated.
  • Ｂ）If the policy holder's main intentions were to get the Insurance money.
  • Ｃ）If you Involve In the act of violence, suicide, and criminal.
  • Ｄ）If the insured has Brain disease or Insanity or any similar diseases.
  • Ｅ）If the Insured had a surgical operation childbirth due to pregnancy, premature birth, or abortion, other medical procedure.
  • Ｆ）If accidents happen due to natural disasters like volcano eruption, earthquake or due to the tsunami.
  • Ｇ）Due to War, armed foreign act, revolution, civil war, riot of incident, similar to these.
